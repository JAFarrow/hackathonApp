Welcome to FlashMash!

FlashMash is a simple solution to creating flashcards for studying without the papercuts.

Why Flashcards?

Flashcards are proven to improve information retention.

While looking at the question side, you are training the parts of your brain responsible for active recall.

Hover over your cards..

..and reveal the answer!

With the answer so close by, this process reinforces the link between the question and answer immediately.

How FlashMash works.

Simply enter your question and answer below and your flashcard will be added to your deck.

Please Note: Cards will disappear when your session expires.
